Police shootings: Deputies shielded by the badge

In an unrivaled look at police shootings in Palm Beach County and the Treasure Coast since 2000, The Palm Beach Post and WPTV NewsChannel 5 detail more than 250 incidents of officers firing guns. The shootings killed 86 people and wounded 89. Almost all of the shootings were deemed to be just.
